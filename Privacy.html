Your Privacy

At BrightNews, we focus on positivity. That applies to your data too. We only process what is strictly necessary to provide our service.

  • Subscriptions: For "Glow" and "Shine" members, we process payment details via Ko-fi. We do not store credit card numbers on our own servers.
  • Data Storage: Your data is hosted on Strato servers within the EU. We have a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) to ensure your privacy is guaranteed.
  • Zero Third-Party Sales: We never sell, trade, or leak your data to advertisers.
  • Your Rights: Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, or delete your data at any time. Send a request to our support email.

Cookies & Technology

BrightNews uses functional cookies and local storage to remember your language preferences and login session. We use minimal, privacy-friendly analytics to improve the site. For any tracking beyond functional necessity, we will always ask your permission first.

AI & Accessibility

Our news is generated by AI. We believe in transparency: every article includes an AI-disclaimer. Furthermore, we are committed to the WCAG 2.1 AA standards to ensure that positive news is accessible to everyone, regardless of disability.
